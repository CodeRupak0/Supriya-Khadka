Title: Gen-Z Protest
Date: 2082 Bhadra
Author: — Supriya Khadka

Content:
Every revolution comes with a cost, often claiming lives from both sides: protesters and government forces alike. Innocent civilians became victims in this struggle, yet out of the ashes, a new hope has risen, much like a phoenix. Amidst the destruction and chaos, it wasn’t only the Gen-Z protesters involved; opportunistic groups and vested interests also fueled the vandalism for their own gain. Now, as a new government prepares to form, the Nepali people’s hope is that corruption will finally be eradicated.

Major Learnings from the Gen-Z Protest
 • Revolution brings transformation: True revolutions demand sacrifice: destruction, chaos, and even loss of lives. These moments of violence become catalysts for social change.
 • Change doesn’t always take decades: When injustice boils over, transformation can erupt swiftly. It took only two days for the entire nation to flood the streets and raise their voice.
 • Zero tolerance for injustice: Gen-Z has made it clear that they will not accept corruption, abuse of power, or any form of injustice in the country.
 • Opportunistic groups will exploit weak control: In times of instability, self-serving groups will always try to rise by taking advantage of the situation.

This uprising will remain imprinted in the hearts of Nepali people forever. From now on, no leader or government official will dare to engage in corruption without fearing the consequences. Every personnel will think twice before taking illegal actions.
Now we only hope these dark days will never return, and that the need for such a painful revolution will not arise again.
